Modeling for the future

With the power of computational chemistry and modeling one can see and explain many phenomena beyond what is possible with modern experimental techniques. Not only can a significant amount of time and money be saved with theroetical approaches, but also risks both for the scientist and the environment. There is little doubt that great parts of the future in molecular sciences lie within the endless possibilities of theoretical chemistry and modeling.
